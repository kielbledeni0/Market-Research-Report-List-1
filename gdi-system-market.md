<p><p>The Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) system market encompasses several key components: fuel injectors, engine control equipment, sensors, fuel rails, and fuel pumps. </p><p>Fuel injectors directly deliver fuel into the combustion chamber, enhancing efficiency. Engine control equipment manages the overall operation of the engine. Sensors monitor various parameters for optimal performance. Fuel rails distribute fuel to injectors, ensuring consistent flow. Fuel pumps provide the necessary pressure to deliver fuel from the tank to the engine, directly impacting performance and emissions control.</p></p>
