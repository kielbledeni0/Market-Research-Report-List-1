<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Dental scalers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive Ultrasonic Scalers</li><li>Piezoelectric Ultrasonic Scalers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic dental scaler market is categorized into two main types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric ultrasonic scalers. Magnetostrictive scalers utilize a magnetic field to vibrate and remove plaque, operating at a frequency of 25,000 to 30,000 Hz. In contrast, piezoelectric scalers use electric signals to produce ultrasonic vibrations, typically operating at frequencies ranging from 28,000 to 36,000 Hz. Both types enhance dental cleaning efficiency and patient comfort, with different mechanisms and applications tailored to diverse dental practices.</p></p>
