<p><p>The automotive torque tools market encompasses various types, each serving distinct purposes. Torque screwdrivers precisely tighten screws to specified torque values, ensuring optimal connection without damage. Torque wrenches measure and apply torque accurately, crucial for assembly and repair tasks. Torque multipliers amplify input torque, enabling easier fastening in high-torque applications. Nutrunner tools provide controlled torque for fastening nuts and bolts, enhancing efficiency and consistency. Together, these tools play a vital role in automotive maintenance and assembly processes, ensuring safety and reliability.</p></p>
