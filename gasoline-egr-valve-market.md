<p><ul><li>Low Pressure</li><li>High Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gasoline EGR valve market is divided into two primary types: low pressure and high pressure. Low pressure EGR valves operate by recirculating exhaust gases back into the intake manifold at lower pressures, helping to reduce emissions and improve fuel efficiency in lighter loads. High pressure EGR valves, on the other hand, function by introducing exhaust gases at higher pressures, making them suitable for high-performance engines, enhancing combustion efficiency, and further lowering emissions during heavier loads.</p></p>
