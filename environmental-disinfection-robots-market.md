<p><p>The Environmental Disinfection Robots market features two primary types: UV-C robots and HP( Hydrogen Peroxide) robots. UV-C robots utilize ultraviolet light to inactivate microorganisms by damaging their DNA, making them effective for disinfecting surfaces quickly and thoroughly. HP robots, on the other hand, disperse hydrogen peroxide vapor to achieve a deep-level disinfection, effectively penetrating complex surfaces and materials. Both technologies are increasingly adopted in healthcare, hospitality, and public spaces to enhance sanitation and prevent the spread of infections.</p></p>
