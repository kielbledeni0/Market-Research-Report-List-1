<p><strong>The Lighting Capacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Type Lighting Capacitor</li><li>Oil Filled Type Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lighting capacitor market primarily includes two types: dry type and oil-filled type capacitors. Dry type lighting capacitors are encapsulated in a solid resin, offering reliability and minimal maintenance, making them ideal for indoor applications. In contrast, oil-filled capacitors utilize oil as a dielectric medium, providing excellent thermal management and high capacitance, suitable for outdoor or high-voltage environments. Both types serve to enhance power factor correction and improve energy efficiency in lighting systems, catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
