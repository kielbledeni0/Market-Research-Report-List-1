<p><p>Cardiovascular monitoring and diagnostic devices encompass various tools used to assess heart health. ECG systems provide real-time heart rhythm analysis, while Holter monitors continuously record heart activity over 24 to 48 hours for detailed review. Event monitors, used for intermittent monitoring, activate when patients experience symptoms. Implantable loop recorders offer long-term cardiac monitoring, often for months, to detect irregularities. Other devices may include smartphone-integrated monitors and wearable sensors, enhancing accessibility and patient engagement in heart health management.</p></p>
