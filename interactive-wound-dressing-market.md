<p><p>The interactive wound dressing market consists of various types, including semi-permeable films, semi-permeable foams, and hydrogels. Semi-permeable films provide a protective barrier while allowing moisture vapor exchange, maintaining a moist wound environment. Semi-permeable foams absorb exudate while cushioning the wound and preventing irritation. Hydrogels offer moisture for dry wounds, promoting healing by maintaining a hydrated environment. Each type caters to specific wound characteristics, enhancing healing processes and improving patient comfort.</p></p>
