<p><p>The fiberglass yarn market comprises primarily two types: fiber yarn and fiber roving. Fiber yarn is a continuous strand of fiberglass used in textiles, composites, and various industrial applications, providing strength and heat resistance. Fiber roving, on the other hand, consists of loose strands of fiberglass fibers that can be formed into a mat or used in composite materials, allowing for easy handling and enhanced bonding. Both types are crucial for applications requiring durability and lightweight properties in construction and manufacturing.</p></p>
