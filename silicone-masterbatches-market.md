<p><p>The silicone masterbatch market comprises several types, each serving specific functions in the plastics industry. White masterbatch contains titanium dioxide for opacity and brightness, while black masterbatch incorporates carbon black for color and UV protection. Color masterbatch is used to introduce various hues, enhancing aesthetic appeal, while additive masterbatch includes concentrations of functional additives like stabilizers and antioxidants to improve performance. Together, these masterbatches facilitate custom plastic formulations tailored to specific application needs.</p></p>
