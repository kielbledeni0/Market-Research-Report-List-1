<p><strong>The AMOLED Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AMOLED market is primarily segmented into two types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diodes (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLEDs (PMOLED). AMOLED displays utilize an active matrix of OLED pixels, allowing for high refresh rates, better color depth, and energy efficiency, making them popular in smartphones and high-end displays. In contrast, PMOLED technology employs a simpler design with lower resolution and refresh rates, suited for smaller screens and less demanding applications like wearables and simple displays.</p></p>
