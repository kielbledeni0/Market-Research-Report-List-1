<p><strong>The Automotive Gudgeon Pin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Pin</li><li>Full Floating Pin</li><li>Semi-Floating Pin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive gudgeon pin market features three primary types: fixed pins, full floating pins, and semi-floating pins. Fixed pins are rigidly mounted in the piston, offering enhanced stability but limited motion. Full floating pins are designed to move freely within both the piston and the connecting rod, reducing friction and wear. Semi-floating pins combine elements of both, allowing some degree of movement while remaining partially fixed. Each type is chosen based on engine design, performance requirements, and manufacturing costs.</p></p>
