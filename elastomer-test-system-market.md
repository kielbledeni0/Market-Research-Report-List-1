Compression testing assesses the material's behavior when subjected to compressive forces, crucial for applications like seals and gaskets. Adhesion testing evaluates the bond strength between elastomers and other materials, vital for ensuring reliable performance in multi-material assemblies. Each testing type plays a critical role in quality assurance and product development.</p></p>
