<p><p>The Multi GNSS Receiver Chips market is segmented into two main types: Single Frequency and Dual Frequency. Single Frequency chips utilize signals from one frequency band, making them cost-effective and suitable for basic applications like automotive navigation and personal tracking. In contrast, Dual Frequency chips receive signals from two frequency bands, enhancing accuracy and reliability, particularly in challenging environments. They are ideal for advanced applications such as surveying, agriculture, and autonomous vehicles, where precision is critical for performance.</p></p>
