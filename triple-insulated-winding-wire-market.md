<p><p>Triple Insulated Winding Wire (TIW) is categorized into two main types: Standard Type and Self Bonding Type. The Standard Type features three layers of insulation, enhancing safety and performance in electrical applications. This is ideal for traditional winding processes. In contrast, Self Bonding Type TIW incorporates a unique adhesive layer that allows the wire to bond to itself during winding, simplifying assembly and providing reliable insulation. Both types cater to diverse needs in industries such as motors, transformers, and inductors.</p></p>
