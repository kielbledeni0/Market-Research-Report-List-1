<p><strong>The Digital Identity Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biometrics</li><li>Non-biometrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital identity solutions encompass two primary market types: biometrics and non-biometrics. Biometrics involve the use of unique physical traits, such as fingerprints, facial recognition, or iris scans, to verify an individual's identity, providing a high level of security and convenience. In contrast, non-biometrics utilize traditional methods like passwords, PINs, or security questions, relying on knowledge-based verification. While biometrics offer advanced protection against identity theft, non-biometrics are often easier to implement and manage in various applications.</p></p>
