<p><p>The meat protein analysis equipment market includes various technologies, prominently DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy). DEXA technology provides precise measurements of bone and soft tissue, enabling accurate protein content assessment in meat. In contrast, NIR technology utilizes light reflectance to quickly analyze protein and fat levels through non-destructive methods, making it suitable for real-time processing. Both technologies enhance quality control in meat production, ensuring accurate nutritional labeling and compliance with industry standards.</p></p>
