<p><p>The insulated windows market comprises several key types designed to improve energy efficiency. Secondary glazing adds an extra layer of glass, enhancing thermal insulation. Tinted glass reduces solar heat gain and glare. Double and triple glazing feature multiple glass panes, providing superior insulation. Low-emissivity coating glazing minimizes heat transfer by reflecting infrared radiation while allowing light in. Reflective coating glazing reflects sunlight, further reducing heat absorption. Together, these options offer diverse solutions for energy-efficient building design.</p></p>
