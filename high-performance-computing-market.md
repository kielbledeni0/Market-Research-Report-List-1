<p><p>The High-Performance Computing (HPC) market can be divided into two primary types: On-Premises and Cloud. On-Premises HPC involves dedicated hardware and infrastructure located within an organization's facilities, offering enhanced control and security but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud HPC leverages virtualized resources and services provided by external providers, offering scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. With Cloud HPC, organizations can access powerful computing capabilities without the need for extensive physical infrastructure, enabling rapid deployment and resource allocation.</p></p>
