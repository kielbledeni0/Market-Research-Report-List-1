<p><p>The omnidirectional Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) market is characterized by two primary guiding systems: optical guiding and magnetic guiding. Optical guiding utilizes cameras and sensors to follow visual markers, allowing for flexible navigation in dynamic environments. Conversely, magnetic guiding employs magnetic tracks or markers embedded in the floor, directing AGVs along predetermined paths with high precision. Both systems cater to diverse industry needs, optimizing material handling and enhancing operational efficiency while providing varying levels of adaptability and infrastructure requirements.</p></p>
