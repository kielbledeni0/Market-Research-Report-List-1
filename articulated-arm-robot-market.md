<p><strong>The Articulated Arm Robot Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transversely Articulated</li><li>Longitudinal Articulated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Articulated arm robots are classified into types based on their articulation patterns: transversely articulated and longitudinally articulated. Transversely articulated robots feature joints that allow movement primarily across a horizontal plane, enhancing reach and flexibility in tasks like assembly and welding. In contrast, longitudinally articulated robots focus on vertical movement, making them suited for tasks requiring precision in height adjustments, such as painting or material handling. Each type addresses specific industrial needs, optimizing performance and efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
