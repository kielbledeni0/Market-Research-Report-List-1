<p><p>The MRI safe biopsy needle market encompasses three primary types: Fine Needle Biopsy (FNB), Core Needle Biopsy (CNB), and Vacuum Assisted Biopsy (VAB). FNB utilizes a thin needle for sampling small tissue portions, suitable for superficial lesions. CNB employs a larger needle to obtain larger tissue samples, enhancing diagnostic accuracy. VAB leverages a vacuum mechanism to retrieve multiple tissue samples efficiently, often used for larger or deeper lesions. These MRI-compatible devices ensure patient safety during imaging procedures.</p></p>
