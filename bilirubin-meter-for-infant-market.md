<p><p>The bilirubin meter for infants is crucial for monitoring jaundice. It has two primary market types: transcutaneous and bench-top devices. Transcutaneous bilirubin meters estimate bilirubin levels non-invasively through the skin, providing quick results without blood draws. They are portable and user-friendly, ideal for bedside or outpatient settings. Bench-top bilirubin meters, on the other hand, require blood samples to measure bilirubin levels more accurately in a lab setting. They are typically used for confirming diagnoses and managing treatment plans.</p></p>
