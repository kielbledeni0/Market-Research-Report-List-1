<p><p>The gypsum centrifuge market is primarily divided into two types: horizontal and vertical centrifuges. Horizontal centrifuges are designed with a horizontal rotor, facilitating efficient separation of materials and often exhibiting higher throughput capacity. In contrast, vertical centrifuges utilize a vertical rotor, which enhances sedimentation rates and can be more effective in handling slurries with varying viscosities. Both types play crucial roles in processing gypsum by enabling efficient dewatering and separation, catering to different operational needs and preferences within the industry.</p></p>
